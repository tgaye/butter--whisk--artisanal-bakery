Our Story

Founded in 2015, Butter & Whisk began as a small home bakery with a passion for creating exceptional pastries using traditional techniques and premium ingredients.

Our founder, a classically trained pastry chef, wanted to bring the artistry of French patisserie to the local community with a modern twist.

Every item we create is made from scratch daily, with no shortcuts or preservatives. We source our ingredients locally whenever possible, supporting sustainable farming practices.

What started as a weekend farmers market stand has grown into a beloved neighborhood bakery, but we've never lost sight of our commitment to quality and craftsmanship.
